About Us

Engineering Excellence in Sheet Metal

With engineering roots dating back to 2013 and formally established as PSI PHI INDIA Engineering & Trading in 2019, we are a premier sheet metal manufacturing company based in the GT Road Industrial Area, Ghaziabad. Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Vikas Sharma, we have grown into a trusted manufacturing partner for leading companies in the elevator and industrial sectors.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with advanced CNC machinery, precision laser cutters, hydraulic press brakes, and certified welding stations — enabling us to deliver components that meet the highest standards of quality and precision.

What We Do

Our Core Services

Comprehensive sheet metal fabrication capabilities powered by advanced machinery and skilled craftsmanship

Laser Cutting

High-precision CNC fiber laser cutting for complex geometries with tolerances up to ±0.1mm. Ideal for intricate parts and rapid prototyping.

Bending

CNC hydraulic press brake bending for consistent angle forming — box forms, channels, and custom brackets with repeatable precision up to ±0.5°.

Welding

Expert MIG, TIG, and spot welding by certified welders ensuring structural integrity. Specialized in joining sheet metal for elevator and industrial components.

Powder Coating

Electrostatic powder coating for a durable, corrosion-resistant finish. Eco-friendly dry finishing process with uniform coverage and a wide range of colors & textures.

Clinching

Mechanical clinching technology for joining sheet metal without consumables — producing clean, strong joints with no heat distortion or surface damage.

Laser Welding

Fiber laser welding for deep, narrow, high-strength welds with minimal heat distortion — ideal for mild steel, stainless steel, and aluminium on thin, intricate components.

Spot Welding

Seamless laser spot welding that joins overlapping sheets at precise points without filler — leaving a clean, mark-free finish ideal for visible panels and thin gauges.
