Our Services

State-of-the-art manufacturing processes powered by precision CNC machinery, skilled technicians, and rigorous quality control at every stage.

01

Laser Cutting

CNC Fiber Laser Technology

Our CNC fiber laser cutting systems deliver unmatched precision for complex geometries and intricate part designs. We process mild steel, stainless steel, and aluminum with tight tolerances up to ±0.1mm, ensuring every component meets exact engineering specifications.

  • Precision tolerances up to ±0.1mm
  • Complex geometry & intricate profiles
  • Mild steel, stainless steel & aluminum
  • Minimal material waste with optimized nesting
02

Bending

Hydraulic Press Brake Forming

Hydraulic press brake bending delivers accurate angles and consistent bends across production runs. Our multi-axis CNC bending capabilities handle complex multi-bend profiles, box forms, and channel sections with repeatable precision for structural and cosmetic applications alike.

  • Multi-axis CNC press brake forming
  • Accurate angles with consistent repeatability
  • Box forms, channels & complex profiles
  • Suitable for structural, machinery & household parts
03

Welding

MIG / TIG / Spot Welding

Our certified welders deliver structurally sound joints using MIG, TIG, and spot welding processes. Every weld undergoes visual and dimensional inspection to ensure structural integrity, load-bearing capacity, and surface finish quality demanded by critical elevator and industrial applications.

  • MIG, TIG & spot welding capabilities
  • Certified welders with quality inspection
  • High structural integrity & load-bearing joints
  • Clean surface finish for cosmetic applications
04

Clinching

Mechanical Fastening Technology

Clinching provides clean, strong mechanical joints without consumables like rivets, screws, or welding wire. This cold-forming process creates reliable sheet-to-sheet connections ideal for applications requiring a smooth, mark-free surface finish and consistent joint strength across high-volume production.

  • No consumables — no rivets, screws, or wire
  • Clean, mark-free surface finish
  • Reliable sheet-to-sheet mechanical joints
  • Ideal for high-volume production runs
