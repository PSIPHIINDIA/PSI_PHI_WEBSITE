About Us

Engineering Excellence in Sheet Metal

Established in 2019, PSI PHI INDIA Engineering & Trading is a premier sheet metal manufacturing company based in the Industrial Area of South Lalkuan, Ghaziabad. Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Vikash Sharma, we have grown into a trusted manufacturing partner for leading companies in the elevator and industrial sectors.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with advanced CNC machinery, precision laser cutters, hydraulic press brakes, and certified welding stations — enabling us to deliver components that meet the highest standards of quality and precision.

What We Do

Our Core Services

Comprehensive sheet metal fabrication capabilities powered by advanced machinery and skilled craftsmanship

Laser Cutting

High-precision CNC fiber laser cutting for complex geometries with tolerances up to ±0.1mm. Ideal for intricate parts and rapid prototyping.

Bending

High-accuracy CNC hydraulic press brake bending for consistent angle forming across every production run. From single bends to complex multi-stage profiles — box forms, channels, and custom brackets — we deliver repeatable precision with tolerances up to ±0.5°.

Welding

Expert MIG, TIG, and spot welding by certified welders ensuring structural integrity. Specialized in joining sheet metal for elevator and industrial components.

Clinching

Mechanical clinching technology for joining sheet metal without consumables — producing clean, strong joints with no heat distortion or surface damage.
